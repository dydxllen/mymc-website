Who Are We?

MYMindComrade is dedicated to breaking the stigma around mental health and providing resources and support to those in need.

Our Vision

A world where mental health is prioritized, understood, and supported without stigma or judgment.

Our Mission

To educate, advocate, and provide resources for mental health awareness while building a supportive community for those affected by mental health challenges.

Our Advocacy Pillars

We focus our efforts through three key initiatives to create meaningful impact in mental health advocacy.

FundaMental

Providing fundamental education and resources about mental health, breaking down complex topics into accessible information for everyone.

Hi2U Mental Health Initiative

Creating spaces for meaningful connections and conversations about mental health, fostering a community of understanding and support.

The Mind Tribune

Sharing stories, research, and insights about mental health experiences to increase awareness and reduce stigma through powerful narratives.
